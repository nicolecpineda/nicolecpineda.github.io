About Me

Hey there! I’m Nicole, a senior at Columbia University majoring in Computer Science & Mathematics. I'll be graduating and getting my bachelor's degree in April 2021. I’m currently looking to join a company of any size as a Software Developer, and join a team of talented and creative individuals that I can collaborate with to build interactive and beautiful applications.

Throughout my academic career, internship experiences, and some self-teaching, I’ve developed a broad set of skills that I work to improve every day. Here are some of them:

  • I mainly work in front-end development with JavaScript and jQuery or React, but I also have experience on the back-end with Python and PHP.
  • I’ve worked with relational databases using mainly PostgreSQL.
  • I enjoy dabbling in design: I am familiar with all the prototyping stages, from paper & pen to Figma. All websites and applications I’ve built are fully responsive with CSS and Bootstrap.
I am a quick learner, so if I have to learn anything new, I’ll do so efficiently!

When I’m not coding, I love to cook for my friends and family, as well as paint with acrylics, which has been a hobby of mine since childhood.

Check out my LinkedIn and my GitHub, where you can see the code for this website and some of my projects above!
